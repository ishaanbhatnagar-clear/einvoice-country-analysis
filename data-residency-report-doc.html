Data Center Strategy Summary

Local Data Centers Required (5 countries)

  • Saudi Arabia: STC Cloud, Mobily, Oracle KSA
  • Turkey: Turkcell, Vodafone Turkey
  • Poland: KSeF (govt system) - keep backups in EU
  • Luxembourg: LuxConnect, Ebrc, POST Luxembourg
  • Tunisia: TTN or local with authorization

Specific AWS Regions Required (3 countries)

  • UAE: me-central-1
  • Israel: il-central-1
  • France: eu-west-3 (verify SecNumCloud certification)

Central EU Hub Strategy

  • Primary Hub: eu-central-1 (Frankfurt) or eu-west-1 (Ireland)
  • Southern Europe: eu-south-1 (Milan)
  • Nordic Countries: eu-north-1 (Stockholm)
  • Iberian Peninsula: eu-south-2 (Spain)

Fully Flexible - Any Region (13 countries)

UK, Estonia, Egypt, Oman, Jordan, Qatar, Bahrain, Kuwait, Iraq, Austria, Denmark, Greece, Czech Republic, Slovakia, Croatia, Bulgaria, Latvia

Recommendation: Use me-south-1 for Middle East, eu-central-1 for Europe
